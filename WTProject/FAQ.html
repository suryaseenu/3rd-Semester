Frequently Asked Questions


I missed the delivery day. What do I do?

Our delivery assistant will deliver your device within the next working day. You will be notified of the new delivery date through email.


The delivery of my order is delayed. What do I do?

Our delivery assistant will contact you in the case of a delay in the delivery of your device. If you have not been contacted please contact customer care.


If I order for a replacement, when will I get it?

You will receive an email regarding the date on which your replacement will arrive.
